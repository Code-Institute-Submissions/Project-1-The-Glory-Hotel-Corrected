About Us

Welcome to the Glory Hotel! We are one of the leading standard hotels in Berlin. With extensive experience in the hospitality and tourism industry, we are registered as a three-star hotel with the German Tourist Board. Tourists from all around the world are most welcome to our lovely property. Our staff is fluent in English, French, Japanese, Italian, Chinese, and Arabic. We offer over 100 rooms, including standard, studio, and suite options. Our hotel is located in the heart of Berlin, close to many tourist attractions such as the Brandenburg Gate, Reichstag Building, Charlottenburg Palace, Berlin TV Tower, and Berlin Wall Memorial. Additionally, we provide an Airport Shuttle Service exclusively to Berlin Airport. The hotel is 500m away from Berlin City Center and 700m from the main train station.
